-------------------------

Frozen Foods:
- Frozen vegetables
- Frozen fruits

Canned Foods (in water or natural juices):

- Canned beans

- Canned vegetables (no added salt/sugar)

Fermented Foods:
- Sauerkraut
- Kimchi
- Pickles (naturally fermented)
- Kombucha
- Kefir
- Miso
- Tempeh
- Natto

Note: This list includes foods that are either unprocessed or have undergone minimal processing such as:
- Removal of inedible parts
- Drying, crushing, grinding
- Fractioning, filtering
- Roasting, boiling
- Non-alcoholic fermentation
- Pasteurization
- Refrigeration, chilling, freezing
- Vacuum packaging

These processes preserve the natural state of the food while making it suitable for storage, safe to eat, or more pleasant to consume.
