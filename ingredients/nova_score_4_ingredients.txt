Note: This list includes ultra-processed foods that are:
- Industrial formulations made entirely or mostly from substances extracted from foods
- Derived from food constituents (oils, fats, sugar, starch, protein isolates)
- Synthesized in laboratories (flavor enhancers, colors, emulsifiers, preservatives)
- Contain little or no whole foods
- Include additives designed to make the final product palatable or hyper-palatable
- Often contain ingredients not commonly used in home cooking
- Designed to be consumed anywhere, anytime
- Heavily branded and marketed
- Often high in energy density and low in nutritional quality

These foods are the most processed category and are typically high in added sugars, unhealthy fats, and salt while being low in fiber, vitamins, and minerals.
