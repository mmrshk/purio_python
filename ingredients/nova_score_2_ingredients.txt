Note: This list includes processed culinary ingredients that are:
- Derived from Group 1 foods or from nature
- Processed by methods including pressing, refining, grinding, milling, and drying
- Designed to be durable and suitable for use in kitchens
- Used to prepare, season, and cook Group 1 foods
- Not meant to be consumed by themselves
- Normally used in combination with Group 1 foods to make freshly prepared dishes and meals

These ingredients serve as the building blocks for cooking and food preparation, providing essential flavors, textures, and cooking properties while maintaining their basic nutritional profile.
